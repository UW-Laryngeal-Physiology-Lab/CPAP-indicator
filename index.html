Instructions:

After you press start, the color indicator will turn green.
While the indicator is green, you will phonate into the tube.
After 5 seconds, the box will turn red for 3 seconds, at which point you will stop phonating.
You will repeat this process 10 times, then take a 30 second break.

After your break, you will repeat the process again 7 more times.
