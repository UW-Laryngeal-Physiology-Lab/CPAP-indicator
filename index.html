Ready?

After you press start, there will be a green box on the screen.
While the box is green, you will phonate into the tube.
After 5 seconds, the box will turn red for 3 seconds, at which point you will stop phonating.
You will repeat this process 10 times, then take a 30 second break.
After your break, you will repeat the above process again 7 more times.
